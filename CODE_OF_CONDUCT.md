# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Umbrix community a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Encouraging collaboration
- Supporting new contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated violations may result in removal from the project or community.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers through the Umbrix community channels.
